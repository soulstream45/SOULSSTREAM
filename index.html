ABOUT US

Soul Stream Network is an inter-nondenominational ministry that holds unto the great commission through the preaching and the teaching of the Word of God . It was established in 2024.It is our mandate to disciple and help men grow into the full stature of Christ.

Founder:

Pastor Chris Boakye Yiadom is a young preacher who has an unquenchable passion for the salvation of souls. He has a strong desire to groom men for the work of Ministry. He has strong teaching that has brought illumination to the souls of many .He is an Apostle by calling
